# AIAS Business Strategy - Internal Document

**Last Updated:** 2025-01-XX  
**Status:** Active  
**Confidentiality:** Internal Only

## Executive Summary

AI Automated Systems (AIAS) is a Canadian SaaS platform that builds custom AI platforms and automation solutions for businesses. We differentiate by building custom solutions rather than just integrations, focusing on Canadian businesses with data residency and compliance requirements.

## Vision

To become the leading Canadian provider of custom AI automation platforms, helping businesses save 10+ hours per week and increase ROI by 40% through intelligent automation.

## Problem Statement

Businesses struggle with:
- Manual, repetitive tasks consuming 10+ hours per week
- Lack of custom AI solutions tailored to their needs
- Data residency and compliance concerns (PIPEDA, GDPR)
- High costs and complexity of enterprise AI solutions
- Difficulty finding Canadian AI development expertise

## Solution

AIAS provides:
1. **Custom AI Platform Development** - Not just integrations, but full custom platforms
2. **Workflow Automation** - Pre-built and custom workflows
3. **AI Agents** - Custom AI agents for specific business needs
4. **Canadian-First** - Data residency, PIPEDA compliance, Canadian support
5. **Proven Results** - TokPulse and Hardonia Suite as case studies

## Target Market

### Primary ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- **Size:** Small to medium businesses (10-500 employees)
- **Location:** Canada (primary), US (secondary)
- **Industry:** E-commerce, professional services, SaaS
- **Pain Points:** Manual processes, need for automation, compliance requirements
- **Budget:** $99-$500/month for automation tools

### Secondary Markets
- Enterprise (500+ employees) - Custom pricing
- International markets - After Canadian market validation

## Revenue Model

### Subscription Tiers
1. **Free** - $0/month
   - 3 workflows
   - 1 AI agent
   - Community support
   - 1,000 API calls/month

2. **Pro** - $99/month (or $990/year)
   - Unlimited workflows
   - 10 custom AI agents
   - Priority support
   - 100,000 API calls/month
   - Advanced analytics

3. **Enterprise** - Custom pricing
   - Everything in Pro
   - Unlimited agents
   - Dedicated support
   - Custom integrations
   - SLA guarantee
   - On-premise option

### Additional Revenue Streams
- One-time setup fees ($99-$999)
- Custom development projects
- Marketplace commissions (future)
- API usage overages
- Training and consulting
