> "2 minutes for onboarding, 30 seconds to create your first workflow. Sarah set up her order automation in under 5 minutes."

## Demo Tips

1. **Keep it simple:** Focus on one use case (Sarah's Shopify automation)
2. **Show, don't tell:** Click through the app, don't just talk
3. **Highlight aha moment:** When workflow executes successfully
4. **Use real examples:** Sarah, Mike, Jessica (personas)
5. **End with value:** "Saves 10 hours per week, $49/month"
