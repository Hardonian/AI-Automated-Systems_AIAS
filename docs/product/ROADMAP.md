## RISK MITIGATION

### Risk 1: Slow Growth
**Mitigation:**
- Multiple acquisition channels (SEO, paid ads, partnerships, referrals)
- Referral program (viral loops)
- Partnership strategy (Shopify, Wave)
- Content marketing (blog, SEO, social)

### Risk 2: High Churn
**Mitigation:**
- Improve onboarding (reduce time-to-value)
- Increase engagement (notifications, emails)
- Better support (FAQ, chatbot, response time)
- Value realization (ROI dashboard, success celebrations)

### Risk 3: Competition
**Mitigation:**
- Unique positioning (Canadian-first)
- Strong brand ("The Canadian automation platform")
- Network effects (more users = more templates = more value)
- Continuous innovation (new features, integrations)

### Risk 4: Technical Issues
**Mitigation:**
- Robust testing (unit, integration, E2E)
- Monitoring (Sentry, Datadog, uptime)
- Quick response (alerting, on-call)
- Redundancy (backups, failover)
